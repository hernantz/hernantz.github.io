Abortion is not a right, it's a wrong

Published on 2021-09-18, under Programming, tagged with database, rest and api.

When we shake hands with someone, we are exchanging electrons, if I rub your shoulder I might be taking away electrons from your body. Am I stealing from you?

In the paradox of teleportation or theseus ship, we face the problem of defining what the esence of something is.

When I say my house, is it the atoms that make the hows that are mine or the idea of a house.

When a plane flies above my house, is it violating my property rights? What if it is a drone? A satellite?

It is hard to define with precision where my rights to own something start, and what they imply.

Yet that doesn't result in the inoperability of property rights. They are very useful!

Now not all of this is consedere trespassing or stealing.

Not all crimes are equally punishable.

Stealing some atoms or your arms is not the same, slaping you in the face or shotting you in the chest are not equally violent. So punishments are different.

Since conception there is a human being. What rights that it have? How punishable is terminating it's life?

A baby cannot cast a vote on elections or buy/sell stuff. Yet he has the right to live.

When in doubt, wouldn't it be better to not kill someone?

There the potential human being, like every one of us is. I am, as I am becoming.
